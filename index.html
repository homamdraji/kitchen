Expert Kitchen Measurement Guide for Cabinet Storage

Before you buy, measure. These pro tips prevent costly returns and ensure every organizer fits perfectly in your new home.

  • Drawer Length Bamboo drawer dividers expand from ~11" to ~17". Measure your drawer's interior length to confirm the range fits before ordering.
  • Cabinet Width Most base cabinets are 12"–24" wide. Check the min/max expansion range on the pots & pans rack before buying.
  • Corner Cabinet Diameter Measure the cabinet interior at its narrowest point. Round down 2" for Lazy Susan spin clearance — usually 9"–12".
  • Pantry Shelf Spacing Measure vertical gaps between shelves. Stacked wire baskets need at least 10"–12" of clearance per tier to stack properly.
  • Sink Width The roll-up rack fits most standard 25"–33" sinks. Measure your sink rim-to-rim width and compare before ordering.
  • Wall Mounting For the magnetic knife strip and paper towel holder, locate a stud or use drywall anchors. Most strips need only 2 screws and hold 10+ lbs safely.
